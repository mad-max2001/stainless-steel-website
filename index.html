About Us

Established in 2010 in the industrial heart of Chennai, Manigandan Enterprises has dedicated over a decade to the art of manufacturing high-quality stainless steel utility products. What began as a small manufacturing unit has grown into a trusted name in household steelware.

We specialize in crafting durable, food-grade stainless steel tiffin boxes, storage containers, and classic layered tiffin carriers. Rooted in the strong manufacturing traditions of Chennai, we combine precision engineering with practical design to ensure every product that leaves our factory is built to last.
